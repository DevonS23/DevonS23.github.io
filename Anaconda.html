Types of Anaconda Hognose Snakes

Conda Snake:

An conda hognose snake is a snake with the anaconda gene from one parent, it reduces the pattern to larger and not as many spots.



Superconda Snake:

An superconda hognose snake is a snake with the anaconda gene from both parents, the pattern is not only reduced but non-existent. They are garenteed to pass on one anaconda gene to all of their babies.
