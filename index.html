Hognose Snakes


Morphs:

A morph is a color or pattern mutation caused by a mutation in a snake's genes, which can be passed on from parents. There are different types of morphs. The hognose snakes I will be showing you are western hognoses, but there are also eastern and southern hognose snakes. Also any type of snake can have morphs.
