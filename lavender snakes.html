Lavender Morph:

The lavender morph is a type of the albino morph, it is the T+ albino type (while standard albinoism is T-) and makes the snake apear purple. While the majority of the snake is a light purple, the eyes, tongue, and belly are dark purple. This morph is the hardest to show on camera because it often looks grey in photos or videos.
